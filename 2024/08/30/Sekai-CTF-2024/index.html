  • In client app, the dummy pin was 000000
  • Initial ideas
    • Use Accessibility Service to collect all the button clicks
    • Use some sort of window overlay
  • After few attempts, we concluded that accessibility service is not enabled
  • Now in terms of window overlay our idea was to
    • Replicate the same layout of the LoginActivity in our window using LayoutInflater , making sure all simulated clicks will be performed on our window, not the challenge app.
    • In OnClickListener of each button, we are collecting the digits
    • Once 6 clicks are performed, we will display the pin in a textview on our window
